**Description:** Allow veterans to input their MOS and instantly see civilian career equivalents.

- "Get Full Career Analysis" CTA (premium upsell)

**Business Impact:** High engagement, viral potential, demonstrates value immediately, natural lead magnet

### Career Path Quiz
**Description:** Interactive quiz to help veterans discover their best civilian career fit.

**Quiz Flow:**
1. Select your branch and MOS
2. What type of work environment do you prefer? (office, remote, hands-on, etc.)

**Business Impact:** High engagement (quizzes are highly shareable), collects valuable data, personalized experience increases conversions
